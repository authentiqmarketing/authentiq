    FAQ

    Common questions.

    What actually happens each month?+
    Once a month we sit down on a 30-minute video call. We come prepared with strategic questions designed to pull out your best thinking. After the call we handle everything: editing, captions, scheduling. Four videos go live on your LinkedIn. You do nothing after the interview.
    What is the free demo?+
    The demo is a short interview session where we ask you a few strategic questions on video. We edit one clip from that conversation and send it to you. No commitment, no credit card. You see exactly what the content looks and feels like before deciding anything.
    How long before I see results?+
    You will have content live within the first week. The real results, warmer sales calls and prospects who already feel like they know you, typically show up around months 3 to 6. This is a long game. The founders who stay with it are the ones who win.
    Will it sound like me?+
    Yes, because it is you. We are not writing scripts or putting words in your mouth. We ask you questions and you answer them in your own words. The content sounds like you because it comes from you.
    How is this different from AI or a ghostwriter?+
    AI and ghostwriters start with a blank page and fill it with something professional but generic. We start with you: your face, your voice, your actual ideas. You cannot fake a face on video. That is what builds trust.
    Is there a contract?+
    No contract. Month to month. We would rather earn your business every month than lock you in.
